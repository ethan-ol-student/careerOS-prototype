Career OS is a two-sided “career intelligence” product. On one side, candidates build direction, evidence, and momentum through an age-adaptive dashboard. On the other, that same signal becomes an employer-facing marketplace where talent is discovered by trajectory, readiness, and fit - not just keywords on a resume.
Its distinctive bet is explainability: every score the product shows - a job match, a readiness level, a fair-pay band, a recommended next move - arrives with the reasons behind it. A second bet is focus on an underserved segment: mid-career professionals (35+), whose accumulated judgement, transferable skills, and track record are largely invisible to tools built for early-career job-seekers.
